Biography

My name is R2-D2, pronounced Artoo-Deetoo and often referred to as Artoo. I am an R2-series astromech droid manufactured by Industrial Automaton prior to 32 BBY. Resourceful and spunky, I have an adventurous personality during my many decades of operation. Along with my counterpart, the protocol droid C-3PO, I constantly found himself directly involved in pivotal moments of galactic history. My bravery, coupled with my many gadgets, played large roles in saving the galaxy time and time again.
